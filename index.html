Me

I am a post-doc at SCI institute under the supervision of Dr. Chris Johnson. My research focus on scalar/vector fields visualization, uncertainty visualization and topological data analysis. I graduated from the department of computer science at the University of Arizona in 2022. My Ph.D. advisor is Joshua A. Levine. My dissertation studied deep learning approaches for exploring collections of visual features of scalar fields.

From time to time, I might also be working on any topics relate to algorithm, graphics, topology, cats, etc.
